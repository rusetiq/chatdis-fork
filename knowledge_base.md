### Required Documents for Admission
- Online Registration Form printout
- Original Emirates ID (Student & Parents) + Copies
- Passport & Residence Visa copies (Student & Parents)
- Attested Birth Certificate

## 4. Fee Policy & Financials
- **Registration Fee:** 5% of the total tuition fee (non-refundable but adjusted against the first term fee).
- **Payment Options:** 2, 4, or 8 installments.
- **Early Bird Discount:** 5% discount if the full year's tuition is paid by April 30th.
- **Sibling Discount:** 5% for the second sibling (10% if paid in one installment).
- **Withdrawal Notice:** Written notice must be provided 30 days in advance.

## 5. Transport & Facilities
- **Transport:** Managed by school buses; fees paid in 4 installments.
- **Uniforms:** Available through the school's approved vendor.
- **Medical:** School is located 0.3km away from NMC Hospital; first-aid available on campus.
- **Technology:** Daily homework and school circulars are shared via the Parent Portal and Mobile App.
